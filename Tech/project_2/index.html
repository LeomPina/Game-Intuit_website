IntuitMaster(IM) -  Decision Analyst

Decision Analyst scores each decision within a game and evaluates its impact on the game's direction. Both on and off-ball decisions are evaluated. Off-ball running is often overlooked compared to passing and dribbling, and can be very time consuming to analyse even if you want to, however, off-ball running is crucial for space utilisation and coverage, and affects the success of every attacking or defending play. In addition, our model doesn't just score decisions, it also predicts what other teams, such as a top team like Chelsea, would do in the same scenario, and this prediction provides a reference for evaluating your team, letting you know if you could have done better, and how you should respond to such scenarios like the top teams do.

  • Win More Games: Make more great decisions during the game
  • Youth Development: Train players in decision-making
  • Scoring Players: Accurately assessing players' decision- making skills
