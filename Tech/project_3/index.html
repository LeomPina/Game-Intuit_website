IntuitMaster(IM) -  Pressure Quantifier

Pressure Quantifier is the first pressure quantification model that gives an accurate quantification of how much pressure individual players and teams are under from their opponents. In a football match, pressure plays a vital role in both attack and defence. In defence, effective pressure can force the opponent to lose the ball. In attack, the ability to make the proper decisions and break the pressure often determines whether or not the opponent's goal can be threatened in that turn.

With the help of the Pressure Quantifier, you can:

  • Improve team performance: analyse whether the team's decisions in attack are effective in defusing pressure, and whether the team's movements in defence are effective in putting pressure on the opponent.
  • Player training: train players to make the right decisions under pressure; train players to make the right moves in defence to put pressure on the opponent.
  • Player scoring: quickly identifying players from transfer targets or youth teams who are skilled in dealing with pressure.
