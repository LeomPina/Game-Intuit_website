IntuitMaster(IM) -  Penalty Expert

In football matches, penalties are a battle between the goal scorer and the goalkeeper. The outcome of each penalty kick can have a significant impact on the direction of the game, and a successful penalty kick shot can often boost a team's ranking in a major tournament. When defending a penalty kick, the goalkeeper makes a judgement call based on the body movement patterns of the shooter through experience from previous matches. Penalty Expert uses AI algorithms to extract and analyse the movement of the shooter from a large amount of data to help the goalkeeper make the best judgement.

Penalty Expert predicts the direction of a penalty kicker's shot before he touches the ball.

  • Improve saving success: understand your opponent's body movement patterns.
  • Improve penalty success: train shooters with AI goalkeepers.
